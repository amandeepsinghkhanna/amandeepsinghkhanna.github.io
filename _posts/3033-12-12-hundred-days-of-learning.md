The pandemic and the lockdown have taken an emotional toll on many. The lockdown was and still is a plight of never-ending meetings, to-do lists and the obvious burnout. A lack of routine and structure, distraction from family, Netflix and the constant anxieties about the spread of the virus have made is extremely difficult to focus on our work and has seriously hampered all plans of personal growth.

<br/>

## What is the 100 days of learning challenge?

The 100 days of learning challenge is my personal spin-off of the 100 days of code challenge, which is popular among programmers. In this challenge, an individual must commit to writing code for one hour each day for hundred days. Since most of the beginners spend their time watching video tutorials and reading articles about concepts that they won't be implementing for months, this challenge helps them kick-start their learning journey with learning and implementing their knowledge at the same time.

Like the 100 days of code challenge, I am committing myself to learn and work on improving my knowledge in the multi-faceted fields of Data Science and Data Engineering. I will be focusing on learning things about various business domains, required programming languages & tools, various machine learning & deep learning algorithms, the required mathematics & statistics and finally try to build as many projects as possible.

<br/>

## Why am I doing this?

I feel like I've reached a plateau in my personal development, and my learning skills have decreased drastically. So here is this challenge that I hope will re-kindle the joy of learning within me. I will be tracking all my progress on the website to be accountable to the goal. I feel motivated to take charge and change things for the better.
